Fonctionnalités Principales

Create and Update a producer page.

A producer, whether a farmer, grower, market gardener, or fisherman, as soon as they sell their products directly to consumers on their farm, will be able to easily create their own webpage to showcase their business. They will benefit from visibility through geolocation, presentation of their activity with photos of the farm, contact details (address and phone number), and a detailed list of the products they sell, including descriptions for each product.

Find producers by geolocation.

Each user of the site will be able to freely search by geolocation, by entering a city and its search radius, or by their geographical point at the time of the search, for producers registered on the application, and view their information page.

Rate and Comment

A user may, if they wish, create a user account that allows them to select their favorite producer(s). They can also assign a rating from 1 to 5 and/or post a comment about a producer regarding their customer experience with them.

About the Project

Our Inspiration

February 2024, the agricultural fair opens its doors. A few weeks earlier, farmers were protesting all over France. Poor remuneration, due to cheaper competition from neighboring countries that do not have as many regulations as France. Regulations can be a good thing, especially when they help improve environmental impact or allow for the cultivation of healthier products for consumers. But why these competitive imports? A farmer testifies: "Unlike other professions, we do not set our own prices, so production costs are higher than the income from the product sold." But what do the French think? The majority support them. It's true that as a French person myself, I am more inclined to choose products that are healthier for me and more respectful of the environment; it's not about ecology, but common sense. Let's think... What if consumers could buy directly from farmers? These events happened a few months before I joined the Holberton School in Laval, events that gave birth to an idea, this project: SAVE AGRI.
